Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna, a classic Italian dish, has become a beloved comfort food around the world. Its layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese create a hearty and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed by the whole family. While it may seem complex, making homemade lasagna is quite straightforward with a bit of preparation. This simple yet detailed recipe guides you through each step, ensuring you achieve a delicious and perfectly layered lasagna. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a kitchen novice, this recipe will help you create a dish that's sure to impress and become a staple in your meal rotation. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to enjoy the ultimate comfort food made right in your own kitchen!

We will begin with listing the ingridients and then get to the instructions on how to cook a perfect homemade lasagna

INGRIDIENTS AND INSTRUCTIONS

For the Meat sauce

Ingridients

  1. 2 kg ground beef
  2. 1 small onion,finely chopped/diced
  3. 2 garlic cloves,minced
  4. 6 finely cut tomatoes
  5. 1-2 satchet of tomato paste
  6. 1/2 cup of water
  7. 2 tablespoons of sugar
  8. 1 teaspoon of dried basil leaves
  9. 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  10. 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  11. 1/4 tablespoon of black pepper
  12. 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsely

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it is browned. Break it up into small pieces with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the chopped onion to the pan and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Mix well.
  5. Add the sugar, dried basil leaves, Italian seasoning, salt, black pepper, and chopped parsley. Stir to combine.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.

While the meat(beef)stew is getting ready now lets prepare the ricotta mixture.

The Ricotta mixture

Ingridients

  1. 6 kg ricotta cheese
  2. 1 egg
  3. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  4. 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsely

Instructions

  • In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and chopped parsley. Mix until smooth and well combined.

    • For the layers

    Ingridients

    1. 12 Lasagna noodles
    2. 7 kg shredded mozzarella cheese
    3. 3/4 grated Parmesan cheese

    Instructions

    1. While the sauce is simmering,bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
    2. Add the lasagna noodles and cook according to the package instructions, typically around 8-10 minutes.
    3. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent them from sticking together. Lay them out on a clean kitchen towel to dry.

    Now that the ricotta mixture and beef stew are ready its time to assemble the lasagna and enjoy the fruits of your labor

    Assembling the lasagna

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    3. Lay 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping.
    4. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    5. Sprinkle one-third of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
    6. Spoon one-third of the meat sauce over the cheese.
    7. Repeat these layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
    8. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.

    Baking the Lasagna

    1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, ensuring it does not touch the cheese (you can use toothpicks to keep the foil off the cheese if necessary).
    2. Bake in the pre heated oven for 45 minutes
    3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges are slightly browned.

    Now that its all done all thats remaining is serving the food and for all to enjoy some sweet hot lasagna

    To Serve

    • Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before serving. This helps it set and makes it easier to cut into neat slices.
