Crispy Homemade Fries

Crispy, golden fries are the perfect side dish for any meal,especially when paired with a homemade burger. Making fries at home allows you to enjoy them fresh and hot, straight from the fryer or oven. This simple yet detailed recipe will guide you through the steps to create perfectly crispy homemade fries every time. With a few key tips and quality ingredients, you can achieve that ideal crunch and flavor that makes fries irresistible. Let's dive into the process of making these delicious crispy homemade fries in your own kitchen!

Ingridients And Instructions

For the Fries

  1. 4 large russet potatoes
  2. 1-2 ltrs of vegetable oil
  3. salt
  4. Optional seasonings: garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, or any favorite seasoning blend

Instructions

  1. Prepare the potatoes

    2. wash and peel the potatoes

    cut the potatoes into uniform sticks about 1/4 inch thick. Try to keep them as even as possible to ensure they cook evenly.

  2. Soak the potatoes

    3. Place the cut potatoes in a large bowl of cold water. Soak for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator. This helps to remove excess starch and keeps the fries from sticking together.

  3. Heat the oil

    4. In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or deep fryer, heat the vegetable oil to 325째F (165째C). Use a thermometer to ensure the temperature is accurate.

  4. First Fry (Blanching)

    5. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with paper towels.

    Working in batches, fry the potatoes in the hot oil for about 3-4 minutes. They should be softened but not browned. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place on a baking sheet lined with paper towels to drain.

    Allow the fries to cool for at least 30 minutes. You can also refrigerate them for up to an hour.

  5. Second Fry (Crisping)

    6. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).

    Again working in batches, fry the blanched potatoes for a second time for about 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.

    Remove the fries with a slotted spoon and place on a baking sheet lined with fresh paper towels to drain. Immediately season with salt and any other desired seasonings.

Now that the cooking is over its time to serve our dish and enjoy some crispy fresh fries

To serve

  • Serve Fries immediately while they are hot and crispy
  • Pair with your favorite dipping sauces such as ketchup,mayonnaise or even choma sauce

Enjoy your perfectly crispy homemade fries! This recipe ensures that each fry is golden and crunchy on the outside, yet soft and fluffy on the inside. Customize your seasonings and sauces to make them your own, and savor the delightful experience of freshly made fries right at home.
